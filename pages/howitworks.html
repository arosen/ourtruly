How does all this work?

When you submit a note for writing on OursTruly, it gets passed to our team of handwriters who transfer your note to ultra high quality stock with their bare hands.

These guys are good; they're the kids who made you look bad in front of your 2nd grade teacher during handwriting practice and make tellers at the bank go "Ooooh" when you hand them a check they wrote.

Who can read my note?

Only the writer at OursTruly is able to view your note so he or she can actually write it. We delete the content of your note after we write it so your message only stays with the recipient you choose to send it to.

Is the handwriting "male" or "female"?

The handwriting style is genderless and timeless.

What if I want a different style? Like more cursive or sloppier?

For now, there is only one style available. But get in touch and we may be able to help.

Where is the note postmarked from?

We put whatever return address on that you specify, but your note will ship from either our office in San Francisco or NYC depending on which is closer to the recipient. The return post stamp isn't obvious to recipients. That said, we're working on adding more sending locations.

Can the sender tell that you guys sent the note?

There is no OursTruly.com labeling on the note or envelope. We only put what you tell us to write.

Is postage included in the price?

Yes, we take care the postage.

What quality stationery are we talking about?

We only use premium stationery from Crane & Co. for our notes.

How long does it take to send the note?

It will take one business day for your order to be processed and shipped.

But how long does a note usually take to arrive?

Once mailed (within 24 hours) a note normally takes 2-3 days to be delivered by USPS.

Do you have bulk rates? Can you do my wedding invitations?

Yes, get in touch and we can discuss details.
